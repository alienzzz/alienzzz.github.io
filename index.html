All About Labrador Retriever

A website which you could learn all about labrador retriever

Labrador Retriever! A mixed breeding from Canada and an origin of New Foundland, a fully friendly dog that is esy to train and like to do sports. And Most importantly, could bring happiness to every person that meets it.

They are more easy to take care of than a golden retriever, and as fun as a hunting dog. Because its original usage of it is to help the owner(a fishermen) to fish, it has developed a webbed toes and two layers of coat with the inside coat waterproof!

Characteristic of Labrador Retriever

These energetic dogs have an excellent, reliable temperament and are friendly, superb with children and equable with other dogs. They crave human leadership and need to feel as though they are part of the family. They are known for sporting and hunting, this breed have totally three colours, black, yellow, and chocolate.

The exercise requirement for the dog is 40 miutes per day, its energy level is averagely good, and it could live up to 12 years there is a low probability that it drool, snore, bark and dig. But it would want attention moderately compared to other dogs.
